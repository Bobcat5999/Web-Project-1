Trading

Forex trading or foreign exchange trading is an easy fast way to make money at home,
it can also be turned into a career by working at an investment banking firm.
The most popular exchange that is made is EURUSD, how it works is you buy euros when the
Value is low, and sell when the value is high, taking your profit in United States Dollars.
There is many strategies and techniques that need to be learned that may take a while,
the most people follow the support and resistance lines and make impulsive trades when
the market is non directional because they are impatient, The very profitable traders wait for
an uptrend or a downtrend and wait for fair value gaps to guarentee a market reversal then use a
4:1 reward to risk ratio to minimize loss and maximize profit.

Preparing to trade the market is easy but time consuming, it involves many hours of backtesting and a
lot of paper trading. But what is that? paper trading is a form of trading where you make no money
and lose none. Trading platforms will give you a balance of 100,000 to trade with. However, this money
doesn't exist. No one profits and no one loses. It's only use is practice and analysis. Backtesting is
is practicing your strategies and learning the market by looking back at the previous market structure.
The market often repeats itself and what worked in the past will often work again.
